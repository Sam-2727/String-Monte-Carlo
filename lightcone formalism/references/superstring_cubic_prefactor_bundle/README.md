Why these four:
- Spradlin-Volovich and Pankiewicz-Stefanski are the direct continuum light-cone sources for the explicit \(K^I \widetilde K^J v_{IJ}(\Lambda)\) coefficient formulas imported by the local helper scripts.
- Dijkgraaf-Motl gives the matrix-string / twist-field interpretation and contact-term discussion.
- Kishimoto-Moriyama-Teraguchi makes the twist-field / three-string-vertex relation more explicit.
